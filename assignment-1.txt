Ans-T.C-> O(sqrt(n)).

Explanation->

	values of a will be 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 , 5........z
	values of b will be 1 , 3 , 6 , 10 , 15 ......n

   	we can observe sum of z terms of a is equal to the zth term of b so

	     =>	 z(z+1)/2  >  n
	     
	     =>  z*z+z > 2*n (ignore lower terms )

	     =>  z*z > n

	     =>  z > sqrt(n).

	     =>  time complexity will be O(sqrt(n)).
